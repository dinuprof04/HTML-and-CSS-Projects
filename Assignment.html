Most Popular Dog Breeds

Labrador Retriever, French Bulldog, and German Shepherd are the most popular dog breeds today.

Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is a friendly and outgoing breed, first recognized by the American Kennel Club in 1917. Labradors have consistently ranked as the most popular dog breed for many years!

French Bulldog

The French Bulldog is a small, playful companion dog, known for its adaptability. The French Bulldog has recently moved up in popularity and has often held the spot as the second most popular breed in the United States.

German Shepherd

The German Shepherd is an intelligent and versatile breed, originally developed in Germany in the late 19th century. Known for loyalty and work ethic, the German Shepherd often replaces older working breeds in modern service roles.
