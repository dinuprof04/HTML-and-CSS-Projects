Steps to Adopt a Dog (Ordered List)

  1. Research dog breeds to find the right fit.
  2. Visit local shelters or adoption websites.
  3. Meet the dogs and ask questions.
  4. Submit an adoption application.
  5. Bring your new dog home and provide care!
